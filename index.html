Taking place at this mythical location, in the heart of Almada, with room for over 120 visitors.

Competitions & Showcases

We strive for higher attending participation from competitive and non competitive participants alike.

Traditional demoscene competitions, aimed at the more competitive natured demosceners

Novelty showcase sessions, where you can premiere your art without voting involved

You create art with computers? We want you to come present it at Inércia!

  • Retro Corner

    We have a few rows of tables reserved for retro computers where you can learn about the old machines you used to have (or never did), watch some demos, play some games and chat with people that can help you get your old computer operational again.

  • Shader Royale

    The world's largest simultaneous online shader programming event, with over 25 live participants from all corners of the world is hosted at Inércia for the 4th year since it's inception.

  • Seminars

    Expect several talks, roundtables and workshops to take place during the event. Covering a range of topics related to the demoscene, electronic tinkering and the digital arts.
