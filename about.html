About Inércia

Digital Art & Music Festival in Portugal, rooted in the demoscene culture, organized yearly on-and-off since 2001 by the Associação Inércia non-profit organization.

Inércia was organized for the first time in 2001. This upcoming 16th edition (the 5th organized in Almada) will be held from the 6th to the 8th of December 2024 at Salão Festas Incrível Almadense, the centenary event hall in the heart of Almada, just across the river from Lisbon. This mythical hall provides us with room for up to 150 visitors (including dedicated table spots with comfortable seating for up to 50 people). The event runs from Friday afternoon until Sunday morning, during which there will be plenty of opportunity to present your work and socialise with other artists, demosceners and hardware tinkerers.

New to the demoscene?

If you have never visited a demoparty before or haven't done so in a long while, this is what you can expect:

  • The focus on Friday is just to arrive, unpack and meet everyone (or finish work on your planned submissions). We will screen some demos on the bigscreen, might also have a talk or workshop planned before dinner. After dinner we will host the 4th edition of the Shader Royale, the worlds largest simultaneous livecoding event, where we will have participants from all corners of the world (some sitting in our hall), programming visuals in realtime for our liveact / DJ sets.
    An afterparty will be arranged at a local nightclub.
  • Saturday mornings are for finishing your submissions or explore the charming downtown of Almada.
    We will have several different talks and events scheduled. Competitions and showcases will begin later in the afternoon, where participants have their productions presented on our large screen with stage and soundsystem. After dinner we will have the highlight of the event: the realtime intro and demo competitions.
    An afterparty will be arranged at a local nightclub.
  • Sunday mornings start slowly with some light socializing activities and culminate with the prizegiving and closing ceremony before lunch. The doors will close with a community lunch amongst the organizers and remaining visitors.
